# AlexNet wins ImageNet

In **2012**, AlexNet — a deep convolutional network from Alex Krizhevsky, Ilya
Sutskever, and [[Geoffrey Hinton]] — won the ImageNet competition by a wide
margin, an outcome widely credited with igniting the modern deep-learning
boom.[^alexnet2012]
