# AlexNet wins ImageNet

On **30 September 2012**, AlexNet — a deep convolutional network from Alex
Krizhevsky, Ilya Sutskever, and [[Geoffrey Hinton]] — was submitted to the
ImageNet Large Scale Visual Recognition Challenge (ILSVRC 2012), which it won by
a wide margin,[^alexnet-wiki] an outcome widely credited with igniting the modern
deep-learning boom.[^alexnet2012]
