# "Attention Is All You Need" published

On **12 June 2017**, Ashish Vaswani and seven co-authors at Google posted
*Attention Is All You Need* to arXiv, introducing the [[Transformer]] — the
architecture that underpins virtually every modern large language model.[^vaswani2017]
