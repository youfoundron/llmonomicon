# ChatGPT launched

On **30 November 2022**, [[OpenAI]] released ChatGPT, a conversational interface
to a fine-tuned [[GPT-3|GPT-3.5]] model.[^openai-chatgpt] It was reported to reach
an estimated 100 million users within two months — among the fastest consumer
adoptions on record.[^reuters-100m]
