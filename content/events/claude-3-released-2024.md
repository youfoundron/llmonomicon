# Claude 3 released

On **4 March 2024**, [[Anthropic]] launched the [[Claude|Claude 3]] family — **Haiku**,
**Sonnet**, and **Opus**, "in ascending order of capability."[^anthropic-claude3][^wiki-claude]
It was the first Claude generation with **vision**: the models "can process a wide range
of visual formats, including photos, charts, graphs and technical
diagrams."[^anthropic-claude3] It was also Anthropic's first claim to frontier parity —
Opus, the largest, was reported to "outperform its peers on most of the common evaluation
benchmarks," including [[MMLU]], [[GPQA]], and [[GSM8K]][^anthropic-claude3] — and it
established the tiered **Haiku / Sonnet / Opus** names Anthropic has used for Claude ever
since.[^anthropic-claude3]
