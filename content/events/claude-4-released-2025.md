# Claude 4 released

On **22 May 2025**, [[Anthropic]] released [[Claude|Claude Opus 4 and Claude Sonnet 4]], a
coding- and agent-focused generation. Both are **hybrid** models offering "near-instant
responses and extended thinking for deeper reasoning," and they can call [[Tool use|tools]]
like web search *during* that extended thinking, alternating between reasoning and tool
use.[^anthropic-claude4] Anthropic billed **Opus 4** as "the world's best coding model,"
with both models posting state-of-the-art **[[SWE-bench]] Verified** scores — Opus 4 at
**72.5%** and Sonnet 4 at **72.7%** — and Opus 4 able to "work continuously for several
hours" on long-running agent tasks, the lineage behind [[Claude Code]] and the 2025
coding-agent wave.[^anthropic-claude4]
