# Claude Sonnet 5 released

On **30 June 2026**, [[Anthropic]] released **Claude Sonnet 5**, the next generation of its
Sonnet-tier model in the [[Claude]] line, succeeding Claude Sonnet 4.6.[^anthropic-sonnet5-whatsnew]
Anthropic calls it "the most agentic Sonnet model yet," able to plan, use tools such as browsers
and terminals, and run autonomously at a level that previously required larger, pricier
models.[^anthropic-sonnet5] It became the default model for Free and Pro plan users, and is also
available on Max, Team, and Enterprise plans, the API, Amazon Bedrock, Google Cloud, and Microsoft
Foundry.[^anthropic-sonnet5][^anthropic-sonnet5-whatsnew] Anthropic says its biggest gains over Sonnet 4.6 are in
coding and agentic tasks, narrowing the gap with Opus-class models like Claude Opus 4.8 at a lower
price — an introductory $2/$10 per million input/output tokens through 31 August 2026, reverting
to a standard $3/$15 afterward.[^techcrunch-sonnet5] It ships with a 1M-token context window by
default, a 128k max output, and is the first Sonnet-tier model with real-time cybersecurity
safeguards.[^anthropic-sonnet5-whatsnew]
