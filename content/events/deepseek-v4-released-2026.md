# DeepSeek V4 released

On 24 April 2026, [[DeepSeek]] released **DeepSeek V4** as an open-weight preview in two
sizes: V4-Pro, a 1.6-trillion-parameter model (49 billion active per token), and V4-Flash,
a 284-billion-parameter model (13 billion active).[^deepseek-v4-preview][^deepseek-v4-hf]
Both ship under the MIT license with a 1-million-token context window and selectable
thinking/non-thinking modes.[^deepseek-v4-preview][^deepseek-v4-hf] More depth on the models is in
[[DeepSeek (models)]].
