# US suspends foreign access to Fable 5 & Mythos 5

On **12 June 2026**, the US government issued an **export-control directive** ordering [[Anthropic]]
to suspend access to **[[Claude|Fable 5]] and Mythos 5** for any foreign national — inside or outside
the United States, and including the company's own foreign-national employees.[^aljazeera-access][^anthropic-access]
To comply, Anthropic **cut off access to both models for everyone** rather than try to partition its
users, while publicly disagreeing: it said it "disagreed that software used by hundreds of millions
of users should be blocked," after a report on the models' code-analysis capabilities appeared to
trigger the order.[^aljazeera-access][^anthropic-access]
