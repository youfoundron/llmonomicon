# Gemini 3 released

On November 18, 2025, [[Google DeepMind]] released Gemini 3, the next generation of its [[Gemini]] model family, led by the Gemini 3 Pro variant made available immediately across Google's products.[^gemini3-blog] Alongside Gemini 3 Pro, Google announced Gemini 3 Deep Think, a higher-intensity reasoning mode that rolled out to Google AI Ultra subscribers after additional safety evaluations.[^gemini3-blog]

At launch, Google reported state-of-the-art or near-state-of-the-art results across reasoning and multimodal benchmarks, including the top spot on the LMArena leaderboard with a 1501 Elo score and 91.9% on GPQA Diamond.[^gemini3-blog][^gemini3-infoq] Gemini 3 Pro shipped with a roughly 1,048,576-token context window and support for text, image, video, audio, and PDF inputs, deployed across Search, the Gemini app, AI Studio, Vertex AI, and Google's new Antigravity development platform.[^gemini3-infoq]
