# GLM-5.2 released

On **16 June 2026**, [[Z.ai]] released **GLM-5.2** as [[Open weights|open weights]] under the **MIT
license** — the roughly 1-million-token-context flagship of its [[GLM]] family.[^glm52blog] On Zhipu's
own coding benchmarks the model edged OpenAI's GPT-5.5 on several long-horizon tasks at roughly
one-sixth the API cost,[^cryptobriefing-glm52] and it was widely received as a sign that an open-weight
model could compete close to the proprietary frontier. (It had reached paid GLM Coding Plan customers a
few days earlier, around 13 June.[^cryptobriefing-glm52])
