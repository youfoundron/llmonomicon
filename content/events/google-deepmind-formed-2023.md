# Google DeepMind formed

On **20 April 2023**, [[Demis Hassabis]] announced that "DeepMind and the Brain team from
Google Research will be joining forces as a single, focused unit called Google DeepMind,"
with Hassabis leading the new lab as CEO.[^gdm-announce] The merger united Google Brain — the
team behind the [[Transformer]] and [[BERT]] — with DeepMind into the single
[[Google DeepMind]] organization that would go on to build [[Gemini]].[^gdm-announce]
