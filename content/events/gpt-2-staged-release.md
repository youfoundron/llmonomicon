# GPT-2 staged release

On 14 February 2019, OpenAI announced [[GPT-2]] but **withheld the full
1.5-billion-parameter model** over fears it could be misused.[^gpt2-announce] It
instead released progressively larger checkpoints across the year, documenting the
reasoning in a study of release strategies,[^solaiman2019] and finally published the
full model on 5 November 2019, reporting "no strong evidence of misuse so far."[^gpt2-release]
The decision opened the modern **release-vs-withhold** debate; depth lives in [[GPT-2]]
and [[Staged and responsible release]].
