# GPT-3 released

On **28 May 2020**, [[OpenAI]] introduced [[GPT-3]], a 175-billion-parameter
[[Transformer]] that demonstrated strong **few-shot learning** — performing new
tasks from a handful of in-context examples without any retraining.[^brown2020]
