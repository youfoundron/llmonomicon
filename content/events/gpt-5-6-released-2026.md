# GPT-5.6 released

On **9 July 2026**, [[OpenAI]] released [[GPT-5|GPT-5.6]] to the public across
ChatGPT, Codex, and the API in three tiers: **Luna**, the cheapest, for
everyday work; **Terra**, for high-volume tasks, which OpenAI says is
competitive with GPT-5.5 at roughly half the cost; and **Sol**, its strongest,
which OpenAI bills as its most capable model yet for cybersecurity
work.[^openai-gpt56][^openai-sol-preview][^wiki-gpt56] The wide release followed
a government-requested limited preview to a small group of partners starting
26 June 2026 and additional testing by the Commerce Department's Center for AI
Standards and Innovation (CAISI).[^engadget][^thenextweb][^cnbc-gpt56]
