# Grok 4.5 released

On **8 July 2026**, [[xAI]] — by then operating under the **SpaceXAI** brand following its
merger with SpaceX — released **Grok 4.5**, the latest version of its [[Grok]] chatbot
model.[^techcrunch-grok45] Elon Musk described it as "an Opus-class model, but faster,
more token-efficient and lower cost," and separately called it "roughly comparable to
Opus 4.7, but much faster" — his own characterization rather than an independently
verified benchmark result.[^techcrunch-grok45]

Grok 4.5 is built on a roughly **1.5-trillion-parameter**, mixture-of-experts **"V9"**
foundation model, trained on tens of thousands of Nvidia GB300 GPUs with a
reinforcement-learning stage covering hundreds of thousands of largely
software-engineering tasks.[^wiki-grok-chatbot] Via the xAI API it is priced at
**$2 per million input tokens** and **$6 per million output tokens**, with a knowledge
cutoff of **1 February 2026**.[^xai-docs-grok45] It launched initially through Grok
Build, the Cursor editor, and the xAI API console, and was not available in the EU at
launch.[^wiki-grok-chatbot]
