# Grok 4 released

On the evening of **9 July 2025**, [[xAI]] launched [[Grok|Grok 4]] — and a heavier,
multi-agent tier called **Grok 4 Heavy** — via a livestream that Elon Musk had
confirmed in advance for 8pm PT.[^teslarati-confirm][^techcrunch-grok4] Rather than
being a single larger model, Grok 4 Heavy runs multiple model instances on the same
problem in parallel and compares their answers before responding, an approach xAI
likened to a study group working through a problem together.[^teslarati-heavy]
Alongside the launch, xAI introduced a new **$300/month "SuperGrok Heavy"**
subscription tier for early access to Grok 4 Heavy and upcoming features.[^techcrunch-grok4]
xAI also claimed state-of-the-art results against rival models on benchmarks
including **Humanity's Last Exam** (Grok 4: 25.4%, Grok 4 Heavy with tools: 44.4%)
and **ARC-AGI-2** (roughly 16%, described as about double the next-best commercial
model).[^techcrunch-grok4]
