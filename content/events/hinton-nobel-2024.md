# Hinton shares Nobel Prize in Physics

On **8 October 2024**, [[Geoffrey Hinton]] was awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics
jointly with John Hopfield, for foundational discoveries that enable machine
learning with artificial neural networks.[^nobel2024]
