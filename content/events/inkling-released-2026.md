# Inkling released

On **15 July 2026**, [[Thinking Machines Lab]] released **Inkling**, its first
in-house model — a [[Mixture of Experts|mixture-of-experts]] transformer with
roughly 975B total parameters and about 41B active per token, a 1-million-token
context window, and pretraining on 45 trillion tokens spanning text, image,
audio, and video data.[^tml-inkling][^techcrunch-inkling] Weights were published
in full on Hugging Face under the permissive **Apache 2.0** license, alongside a
smaller preview companion, Inkling-Small (~276B total / 12B active
parameters).[^tml-inkling][^ghacks-inkling] Coverage framed the release — from
[[Mira Murati|Murati]]'s lab, founded by OpenAI's former CTO — as a deliberate,
open-weights counterpoint to the closed flagship models from OpenAI, Anthropic,
and Google.[^techcrunch-inkling]
