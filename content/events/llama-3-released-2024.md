# Llama 3 released

On **18 April 2024**, [[Meta AI]] released **Llama 3** as open-weight **8B** and **70B**
models, "pretrained on over 15T tokens … collected from publicly available
sources."[^meta-llama3] The bigger milestone came three months later: **Llama 3.1 405B**
(**23 July 2024**), which Meta called "the first frontier-level open source AI model" and
reported as "competitive with leading foundation models … including GPT-4, GPT-4o, and
Claude 3.5 Sonnet," with a **128K-token** context window.[^meta-llama31] It was the
clearest moment of open weights catching the frontier, extending the open-weight thread
from the original [[LLaMA]], [[Llama 2]], and the [[LLaMA weights leak]].[^meta-llama3][^meta-llama31]
