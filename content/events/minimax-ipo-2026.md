# MiniMax's Hong Kong IPO

On **9 January 2026**, [[MiniMax]] listed on the **Hong Kong Stock Exchange** (ticker 0100),
raising about **US$618 million**.[^forbes-minimax-ipo] Shares priced at HK$165 closed up about
109% on debut, giving MiniMax a market capitalization of roughly **US$13.7
billion**.[^scmp-minimax-ipo] It listed a day after rival [[Z.ai]]'s debut, becoming the second of
China's "AI Six Tigers" to go public.[^cnbc-minimax-zhipu]
