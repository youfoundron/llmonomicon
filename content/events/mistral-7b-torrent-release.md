# Mistral 7B torrent release

On 27 September 2023, [[Mistral AI]] released its first model,
[[Mistral 7B / Mixtral|Mistral 7B]], by posting a bare **magnet (torrent) link** on X —
ahead of any blog post — under the permissive **Apache 2.0** license.[^techcrunch][^mistral-7b]
The unceremonious "here are the weights, no strings attached" drop crystallized the
permissive open-weight ethos and became a signature Mistral move, repeated with
Mixtral 8x7B that December. Unlike the earlier [[LLaMA weights leak]], this was a
*deliberate* release rather than an unauthorized one; more depth is in
[[Mistral 7B / Mixtral]].
