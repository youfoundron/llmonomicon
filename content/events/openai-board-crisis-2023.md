# Sam Altman fired and reinstated

Over five days in November 2023, [[OpenAI]]'s board abruptly removed CEO
[[Sam Altman]] on 17 November, saying he was "not consistently candid in his
communications with the board."[^crisis-firing] After president [[Greg Brockman]]
resigned in protest, some 745 of OpenAI's roughly 770 employees threatened to quit
unless the board reversed course, and board member [[Ilya Sutskever]] publicly said
he regretted his part in the decision.[^crisis-wiki] Altman was reinstated on 22
November under a new board chaired by Bret Taylor.[^crisis-return][^crisis-wiki]
