# OpenAI o1 released

On **12 September 2024**, [[OpenAI]] released **o1-preview** — and a smaller
**o1-mini** — to ChatGPT, the first widely available model trained with large-scale
reinforcement learning to produce a long internal
[[Chain-of-thought prompting|chain of thought]], "thinking" before it
answers.[^openai-o1-preview][^openai-o1-reason] OpenAI called it the first of a
series of "reasoning" models; the full **o1** followed on **5 December
2024**.[^wiki-o1][^venturebeat-o1-full] The release opened the inference-time-compute
era of [[Reasoning models|reasoning models]] that o3 and [[DeepSeek-R1]] would
extend.[^wiki-o1]
