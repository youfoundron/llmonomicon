# Pentagon designates Anthropic a supply-chain risk

On **27 February 2026**, the US Department of Defense designated [[Anthropic]] a **supply-chain
risk** and the administration moved to bar federal use of its products, after Anthropic declined to
let the military use [[Claude]] "for all lawful purposes" without limitation — holding to its policy
against "mass domestic surveillance of Americans" and "fully autonomous weapons systems."[^mayerbrown][^mit]
Anthropic sued, and in **late March 2026** a federal court (Judge Rita Lin) blocked the designation
with a preliminary injunction, finding Anthropic "likely to prevail on essentially all of its
theories for why the government's actions were unlawful."[^mit]
