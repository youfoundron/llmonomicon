# PyTorch released

In **January 2017**, [[Meta AI|Facebook AI Research]] publicly released **[[PyTorch]]**
(version 0.1), an open-source, **define-by-run** (eager-execution) deep-learning
framework.[^pytorch-hn] Its Python-first ergonomics drove rapid adoption across
machine-learning research, and it became the substrate on which most modern large models are
trained and served.
