On 2 February 2026, [[Elon Musk]]'s rocket company SpaceX combined with his artificial-intelligence startup [[xAI]] in an all-stock deal, making xAI a wholly owned subsidiary of SpaceX rather than fully merging the two companies' day-to-day operations.[^wiki-xai-2026] The deal valued SpaceX at roughly $1 trillion and xAI at roughly $250 billion, for a combined valuation of about $1.25 trillion — coverage at the time called it the biggest merger of all time.[^fool-spacex-xai-2026][^cnbc-spacex-xai-2026]

xAI had already absorbed the social platform X Corp. in March 2025, before this combination with SpaceX.[^wiki-xai-2026]
