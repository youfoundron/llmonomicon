# Stargate Project announced

On **21 January 2025**, [[OpenAI]], Oracle, SoftBank, and the investment firm MGX
announced the **Stargate Project**, a joint venture intending to invest "up to US$500
billion in AI infrastructure in the United States by 2029" — with **$100 billion**
deployed at the outset — primarily to build data-center capacity for
OpenAI.[^openai-stargate][^wiki-stargate] Unveiled at a White House event, it named
SoftBank and OpenAI as lead partners, with SoftBank taking the "financial responsibility"
and OpenAI the "operational responsibility," and SoftBank's Masayoshi Son as
chairman.[^openai-stargate][^wiki-stargate]
