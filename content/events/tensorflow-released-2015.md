# TensorFlow open-sourced

On **9 November 2015**, [[Google Brain]] open-sourced **[[TensorFlow]]** under the Apache 2.0
license, putting a production-grade, GPU/TPU-accelerated machine-learning framework in
everyone's hands.[^g-tensorflow] It became the dominant deep-learning framework of the late
2010s — and the static-graph incumbent that [[PyTorch]]'s eager-execution model later
displaced in research.
