# Ashish Vaswani

**Ashish Vaswani** is a computer scientist best known as the lead author of
[[Attention Is All You Need]], the 2017 paper that introduced the
[[Transformer]]. At the time he was a researcher at Google Brain.

## Work

The Transformer paper grew out of efforts to improve machine translation without
the sequential constraints of recurrent networks. Vaswani and his co-authors
showed that stacking multi-head [[Attention]] layers was sufficient — and more
parallelizable — than the recurrent and convolutional approaches that preceded
it.

He later co-founded a startup focused on building large models, part of a broad
movement of researchers leaving large labs to commercialize
[[Transformer]]-based systems.
