# Daniela Amodei

**Daniela Amodei** is a co-founder and the president of [[Anthropic]], the AI safety
company she established in 2021 alongside her brother [[Dario Amodei]].[^daniela-wiki]
Where Dario is the company's technical and public face, Daniela is its operational
leader — as president she "leads teams focused on AI safety and model alignment" and
runs the organization day to day.[^daniela-wiki]

## Background

Unusually for an AI-lab founder, Daniela's background is not in machine-learning
research. She came from global health and politics — including work on a congressional
campaign — before joining the payments company **Stripe** as an early employee in
2013.[^daniela-wiki] That operations-and-people background is the complement to Dario's
research one, and it shaped how Anthropic was built as an organization.

## OpenAI and Anthropic

Daniela worked at [[OpenAI]] from 2018, where she "managed the team during GPT-2's
development before moving into safety and policy," ultimately serving as vice president
of safety and policy.[^daniela-wiki] In 2021 she and Dario left, with several other
former OpenAI staff, to co-found [[Anthropic]] — and she has run its operations and
execution ever since; she also sits on the company's board of
directors.[^anthropic-company] The company's mission and structure are covered in its
own entry.
