# Emad Mostaque

**Emad Mostaque** is a British entrepreneur best known as the co-founder and former CEO of
**[[Stability AI]]**, the company behind [[Stable Diffusion]]. A vocal champion of open,
decentralized AI, he was one of the most prominent — and most polarizing — figures of the
generative-AI boom.

## Stability AI

Born in 1983, Mostaque worked as a hedge-fund manager before moving into AI.[^mostaque-wiki] He
co-founded [[Stability AI]] with Cyrus Hodes and led it as CEO, funding and promoting the 2022
release of [[Stable Diffusion]] and arguing loudly that powerful AI models should be open and
broadly accessible rather than locked inside a few large companies.[^mostaque-wiki] He
**resigned as CEO on March 23, 2024**, saying he wanted to pursue decentralized-AI initiatives;
reporting at the time tied his departure to investor pressure over the company's
direction.[^mostaque-wiki]

## Controversy

Mostaque's public profile also drew scrutiny. In **June 2023, Forbes reported** — citing
numerous sources — that he "had misled investors and the public about his educational
background, a partnership with Amazon Web Services and the extent of his involvement in
developing Stable Diffusion."[^mostaque-wiki] Mostaque **disputed the report**, publicly pushing
back on its characterizations.[^mostaque-response] These are contested, reported claims rather
than settled findings, and this entry takes no position on them.

## Since

After leaving Stability AI, Mostaque founded **Intelligent Internet**, a venture built around
his vision of "decentralized" and civic AI.[^mostaque-wiki][^ii-site]
