# Robin Rombach

**Robin Rombach** is a German AI researcher and a central technical figure behind **open image
generation**. He was the lead author of the work that became [[Stable Diffusion]], and is
co-founder and CEO of **Black Forest Labs**, the lab behind the [[FLUX]] models — an
academic-to-industry arc that traces the rise of open text-to-image AI.

## Latent diffusion and Stable Diffusion

During his PhD in the **CompVis** group at LMU Munich, Rombach was lead author of
"High-Resolution Image Synthesis with Latent Diffusion Models" (2022) — the technique, running
diffusion in a compressed latent space, that underpins [[Stable Diffusion]].[^ldm2022] He then
joined [[Stability AI]] as a research director, where he helped scale latent diffusion into the
Stable Diffusion models that brought open image generation to a mass audience.

## Black Forest Labs

In 2024, Rombach left Stability AI with his collaborators Andreas Blattmann and Patrick Esser
to co-found **Black Forest Labs**, where he is co-founder and CEO.[^bfl-wiki][^rombach-bfl] The
lab's [[FLUX]] models became the leading open image generators of the mid-2020s — continuing,
under a new banner, the open-image-generation lineage Rombach helped start at LMU Munich.
