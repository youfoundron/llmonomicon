# Tom Brown

**Tom Brown** is a co-founder of [[Anthropic]]. He is best known for the work he did just before
it: at [[OpenAI]] he was the **lead author of the paper that introduced [[GPT-3]]**, the model
that brought large language models into the mainstream.

## GPT-3

Brown is the first author of "Language Models are Few-Shot Learners" (2020), the paper behind
[[GPT-3]] — the 175-billion-parameter model that showed a language model could take on new tasks
from a few examples in its prompt, "without any gradient updates or fine-tuning."[^brown2020] As
lead author he headed the effort to build and train it; the model itself is covered in the
[[GPT-3]] entry.

## Anthropic

In **January 2021**, Brown was one of seven former OpenAI employees — led by the siblings
[[Dario Amodei]] and [[Daniela Amodei]] — who left to found [[Anthropic]], the AI-safety company
behind [[Claude]].[^wiki-anthropic]
